The Best Movies According to Karthikraghavan

My top 3 movies of all time

Salaar

A gang leader tries to keep a promise made to his dying friend and takes on the other criminal gangs.

Godzilla

The world is beset by the appearance of monstrous creatures, but one of them may be the only one who can save humanity.

Assassin's Creed

Callum Lynch explores the memories of his ancestor Aguilar de Nerha and gains the skills of a Master Assassin, before taking on the secret Templar society.
